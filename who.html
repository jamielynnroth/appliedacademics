Management

Yoshiki Obayashi

Yoshiki founded Applied Academics LLC in 2009. He previously managed investment grade and high yield US and Asian credit portfolios for a proprietary fixed-income trading group of an investment bank in Tokyo. Yoshiki has also been a consultant to major global inter-dealer brokerage firms for the implementation of quantitative equity and credit derivatives models.
